AI Information


AI is a concept that has been around for almost as long as computers. The first digital computer was made in the 1940's, and Alan Turing came up with the idea of the Turing Test in the 1950's, as a way of telling how "human" a computer program is.

How has AI changed in the years since then?

The main kind of AI that is being expanded today is generative AI. GenAI uses references from other pictures, videos and audio to create "original" works.

There are three main kinds of genAI:

Name Function
Variational autoencoders (VAE) Models are given a prompt, then create multiple versions of it. First developed in 2013.
Diffusion models Adds noise to an image, then removes the noise to create responses to a prompt. First developed in 2014.
Transformers Models create extended sequences of content: sentences, full images, or frames in a video. Most popular forms of AI today are transformers, such as ChatGPT or Midjourney.

GenAI has three different phases when it is being developed


Phase Use
Training Creates the foundation for the program.
Tuning Adapts the program to a certain application.
Generation/Evaluation Improves accuracy of results.

While genAI is the most famous form of AI being developed, there are other kinds of AI in use.

NPCsclose

Video games have been using AI for their non-playable characters (NPCs) since the 1980s, with the emergence of games like Pacman. These characters are not controlled by real people, but they use pathfinding and finite state machines to control where they walk and change their behavior, respectively.

AI Advancementsmore_vert
AI Advancementsclose

Programmers are now trying to create more advanced AI with human-like emotions and personalities, as opposed to responses to their environment based on code. Some developers are also trying to incorporate genAI into their games.

Medicineclose

AI is also being used in a variety of industries, such as healthcare. These AI aren't generating new content, but rather detecting patterns or helping find new solutions. Companies like Johnson and Johnson can use AI to detect cancer years before current methods, use robots to assist in complex surgery, or find new treatments for diseases.
